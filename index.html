Yahtzee Rules

  • To save a score, click on the result value.
  • Objective: Score the most points by rolling five dice up to three times per turn to achieve specific combinations.
  • Turn: Roll dice up to three times, keeping any dice after each roll. After rolling, choose a scoring category.
  • Scoring Categories:
    • Aces to Sixes: Sum of dice showing 1 to 6 (e.g., Aces = sum of 1s).
    • Three/Four of a Kind: Sum of all dice if 3 or 4 dice show the same number.
    • Full House: 25 points for 3 of one number and 2 of another.
    • Small/Large Straight: 30/40 points for 4/5 consecutive numbers (e.g., 1-2-3-4 or 2-3-4-5-6).
    • Yahtzee: 50 points for 5 identical numbers. Additional Yahtzees score 100 bonus points if the Yahtzee box is filled.
    • Chance: Sum of all dice (any combination).
  • Bonus: 35 points if the total of Aces to Sixes is 63 or more.
  • Game End: After 13 rounds (one per category), sum all scores. Highest score wins.
